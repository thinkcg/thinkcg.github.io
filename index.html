About

        HongStudio is an independent Unity developer, creating tools and shaders for artists and game developers. Our goal is to provide simple and effective solutions for Unity users.

Assets

  • Texture Merger – This tool can help you to extract the channels of existing textures, reassemble them, and generate new texture resources in the Assets directory.
  • Blend Shader For Polybrush – A shader package for mixed materials.
  • Easy Painter – A mixed material tool for drawing splats in the editor.
